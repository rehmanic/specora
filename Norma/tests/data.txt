
# ✅ **10 CCPA-Compliant Requirements**

### **1.**
The system must provide a clear notice at or before the point of data collection describing the categories of personal information collected.

### **2.**
The system must enable users to submit requests to delete their personal information and process such requests within the statutory timeframe.

### **3.**
The system must offer an accessible method for users to request a copy of all personal information collected about them.

### **4.**
The system must provide a “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” control prominently on the homepage.

### **5.**
The system must support opt-out preference signals (e.g., GPC) and interpret them as valid opt-out requests.

### **6.**
The system must classify and restrict the use of sensitive personal information when a user selects the limitation option.

### **7.**
The system must propagate deletion, correction, and opt-out requests to all service providers and contractors.

### **8.**
The system must verify user identity for access, correction, and deletion requests using reasonable verification methods.

### **9.**
The system must display an annually updated privacy policy containing all required disclosures under the CCPA.

### **10.**
The system must prevent any form of retaliation, such as denying service or increasing prices, when users exercise their CCPA rights.

# ❌ **10 CCPA-Non-Compliant Requirements**

(These intentionally violate CCPA — useful for testing, training, or explaining what *not* to do.)

### **1.**
The system may sell or share personal information without providing an opt-out control.

### **2.**
The system may deny access to users who request deletion of their data.

### **3.**
The system must retain all customer data indefinitely, regardless of purpose or necessity.

### **4.**
The system may refuse to provide users with a copy of their personal information upon request.

### **5.**
The system may collect additional categories of personal information without informing the user.

### **6.**
The system may use sensitive personal information (e.g., location, health data) for advertising without offering any limitation options.

### **7.**
The system does not need to notify third-party service providers when a deletion request is processed.

### **8.**
The system may require users to create a new account to submit CCPA requests.

### **9.**
The system may change the privacy policy without notifying users or specifying effective dates.

### **10.**
The system may reject opt-out preference signals and only accept manual opt-out requests.

### **11.**
Personal data would never be deleted.
